About

Born and raised in Rochester, NY I received my bachelors degree in graphic design. I moved to Indiana June 2018, but haven’t had any luck in securing a job as a graphic designer. In June 2020, as I scrolled through my LinkedIn newsfeed I saw an ad for a coding bootcamp based here in Indianapolis. I visited the website and decided why not gain more knowledge as a designer and perhaps change my career focus into the world of tech, as a web developer. I made the decision of enrolling into Eleven Fifty Academy’s six month web development program. So far, it has been the best, challenging decision I have made in my life. I graduate in February 2021 and will officially be a web developer. As my journey will come to an end with Eleven Fifty Academy, a new one will appear for me, to start my career as a web developer.
